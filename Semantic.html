Facts about dogs:

Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are incredible animals with senses far beyond our own, including a sense of smell that can be up to 100,000 times more powerful than a human's. Beyond their legendary noses, they possess 18 muscles to tilt and rotate each ear, allowing them to hear frequencies twice as high as we can. They don't sweat like humans; instead, they cool down by panting and releasing heat through their paw pads. Remarkably, every dog has a unique nose print, similar to a human fingerprint. These intelligent creatures are as smart as a two-year-old child and can even sense the Earth's magnetic field to align themselves while going to the bathroom.

Often hailed as "man's best friend," dogs are loyal, highly intelligent mammals that have lived alongside humans for over 15,000 years.
